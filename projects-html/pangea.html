pangea network. UI/UX Design
overview.

The Pangea Network project, is a work in progress SMS microlending system created in collaboration with Elixir Labs, a nonprofit that assists other nonprofits in creating free tech products, and Pangea Network. One of the programs hosted by Pangea Network equips women in Kenya with an education on how to start their own business and eventually build financial independence. In order to start a business, Pangea Network offers microloans with low interests to these women. Thus, we are building out the backend admin portal and the SMS messaging system to automate the process of microlending for women in Kenya. This system will increase the efficiency and convenience of taking out, repaying, and tracking microloans.

role.

As the only UI/UX designer on the team, I am currently in the process of creating my first iteration of high fidelity wireframes as we clarify the user experience and the technical aspects of working with short codes.
